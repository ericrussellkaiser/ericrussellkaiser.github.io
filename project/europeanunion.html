European Union

Travel within the European Union is extremely easy.

In contrast to travel to and from the UK, travel within the EU is incredibly easy. No documents other than your ticket is necessary to travel.

Eurostar recommends arriving at least 20 minutes before your train leaves. It is generally a good idea to arrive 30 minutes before for standard tickets, while 20 is good for business tickets. Tickets are also check en route, so keep it where you can easily reach it. If traveling using a special fare (youth fare, senior fare, etc), you will need proof of eligibility.

There are three classes on EU trains; Standard, Comfort, and Premium.
Standard Ticket Features:

  • Basic Wifi
  • 2 bags at 1 piece of hand luggage
  • Access to cafe car (food and drinks not included)
Comfort Ticket Features:
  • All Standard Ticket Features
  • Faster Wifi
  • Larger Seat
Premium Ticket Features:
  • Meals and Drink served at your seat
  • Lounge Access
  • Eurostar Onboard ePress access
