Travel throughout Europe with just one rail ticket!

Includes 33 countries, including all of Western Europe

Are you planning on traveling throughout Europe? Throughout one country? No matter your plan, if you are planning on taking trains in Europe, the EURail pass may be a great option for you.

An EURail pass can get you access to trains throughout Europe, or in just one country, for one fixed price. You can choose a global pass which gets you access to trains in 33 countries, or a cheaper country pass, which you can select from one of 29 countries. There are two main types of passes, flexi and continuous. On flexi passes, you can choose to travel for a certain number of days within a certain period. For example, you can purchase a flexi pass that allows you to travel on 4 days within 1 month. Continuous passes allow for a certain number of consecutive days.

For the majority of trains, you can just hop on and off the train with an EURail pass. You can purchase a reserved seat on these trains, usually for a few dollars, if it is busy or you want to make sure your group can sit together. For example, to reserve a seat on direct train from Amsterdam to Berlin will cost around $9, though it isn't required. Some trains require reservations, though these reservations cost less than if you were to book the train yourself. For example, Eurostar trains require reservations, but cost less through EURail, with the 1:11PM Amsterdam to Paris train on December 5th, 2023 costing $121 through Eurostar, but $38 through EURail. Trains in France, Spain, and Italy usually require reservations, while the Netherlands and Germany rarely do except on night trains.

The EURail website has a fantastic trip planning section. All you have to do is enter your departure and arrival cities and your desired departure time, and the site will tell you how to get there, along with potential costs. The best part is it makes it very easy to control your trip. You can set a max number of transfers, set minimum transfer times, only include trains without reservations, and control the train type.
